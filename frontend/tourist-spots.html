San Fernando

Dinosaurs Island

Family learning attraction with life-sized animatronic dinosaurs teaching about how they lived.

Aqua Beach Club

The Aqua Beach Club is the only club in Asia that offers 2-story cabanas and VIP Table packages with luxurious amenities and features.

Puning Hot Spring

Puning Hot Spring & Restaurant is the thermal springs along Sacobia River where temperatures range from 40 degrees to 70 degrees.

Holy Rosary Parish Church

The Holy Rosary Parish, also known as Santo Rosario Church, today fondly called by locals as "Pisambang Maragul" ("Big Church") stands in the epicenter of the old Culiat town, now Angeles City, Philippines.
